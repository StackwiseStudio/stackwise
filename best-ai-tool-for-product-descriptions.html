Writing product descriptions is one of the most repetitive tasks in ecommerce — and one of the most important for conversions. What to Look for in an AI Tool for Product Description Writing

1

Ecommerce Platform Integration

The best tools connect directly to Shopify, WooCommerce, or your CMS — so you can generate and publish descriptions without copy-pasting between apps. Every manual step in the workflow is a time drain.

2

SEO Optimisation

A beautiful description that nobody finds is useless. Look for tools that let you specify target keywords and automatically structure descriptions for search engine visibility alongside readability.

3

Brand Tone Consistency

If you sell premium goods, your descriptions need to sound premium. If you sell fun products for kids, they need to be playful. Look for tools that let you define your brand voice and apply it consistently across all products.

4

Bulk Generation Capability

If you have a large catalogue, you need a tool that can process products in bulk — ideally by uploading a spreadsheet of product names and attributes, not by entering each one individually.

Step-by-Step: How to Get Started

1

Gather your product attributes before generating

Collect: product name, key features, materials/ingredients, target customer, and price point. The more detail you give the AI, the more specific and persuasive the output. Vague input produces generic copy.

2

Define your brand voice with examples

Paste 3–5 examples of your best existing product descriptions into the tool's brand voice settings. This teaches the AI to match your tone — formal, friendly, luxurious, playful — consistently across every product.

3

Generate, review, and refine a batch of 10 first

Don't immediately generate your entire catalogue. Start with 10 products, review the quality, and adjust your prompts or settings based on what works. This saves time fixing bulk output later.

4

Bulk generate and publish your full catalogue

Once you're happy with the quality, use bulk generation to process your entire catalogue. Review spot-check a random 10% to catch any outliers before publishing everything.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will Google penalise AI-generated product descriptions?

Not if the descriptions are unique, useful, and genuinely describe the product. Google penalises thin, duplicate, or low-quality content — not AI-generated content per se. Always edit AI output to add specific detail only you know about your products.

Can AI write descriptions for technical or specialist products?

Yes — if you give it detailed input. Paste in the technical specifications, target use case, and key features. The AI structures and phrases this into readable copy, but the accuracy of technical detail depends on what you provide.

How long should product descriptions be?

For most ecommerce, 75–150 words for the main description plus 5–7 bullet points for key features. AI tools can generate both formats simultaneously. Longer descriptions work better for higher-priced or more complex products.

Can I use the same AI tool for all my product categories?

Yes — but adjust the tone prompt for each category. A description for a luxury watch needs different language than one for a kitchen gadget. Set up different brand voice templates for different product types.
