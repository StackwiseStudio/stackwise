What to Look for in an AI Tool for Employee Training Materials

1

Content Transformation — Not Just Generation

The best tools don't just generate generic training content — they transform your existing materials. Upload your internal documents, SOPs, product guides, or recorded calls and let AI structure them into proper training modules.

2

Multiple Format Output

Training works best when delivered in multiple formats — video, document, quiz, checklist. Look for tools that can produce the same content in different formats so learners can engage with material in the way that suits them best.

3

Quiz and Assessment Generation

Knowledge without testing is training without accountability. The best tools automatically generate quizzes, assessments, and comprehension checks from your training content — without you having to write every question manually.

4

Delivery and Tracking

Creating training materials is only half the job. You also need to deliver them to employees and track completion. Look for tools that include a basic LMS (learning management system) or integrate with one you already use.

Step-by-Step: How to Get Started

1

List the 10 most important things a new employee needs to know

Before building anything, write down the 10 topics that would make the biggest difference to a new hire's success. These become your 10 training modules. Prioritise ruthlessly — not everything needs to be trained on day one.

2

Record yourself explaining each topic informally first

Before using AI to write formal training content, record a 10-minute voice memo or video of yourself explaining each topic as you would to a new hire. This becomes the source material — much richer than written notes.

3

Use AI to transform your recordings into structured training

Upload your recordings or transcripts to Synthesia or Notion AI. Ask it to: 'Turn this into a structured training module with an introduction, 5 key learning points, 3 practical examples, and a 5-question quiz.' Review and refine.

4

Test on one real new hire before rolling out

Before using AI-generated training with your entire team, test the materials with one new employee and get their honest feedback. What was unclear? What was missing? Update the materials before rolling out company-wide.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can AI-generated training content replace a proper trainer?

For standard process training, product knowledge, and compliance content — yes, AI-generated materials are sufficient. For complex skills, interpersonal coaching, and culture building, human trainers are still essential. Use AI to handle the trainable content so human time goes to the things only humans can do.

How do I make sure employees actually complete the training?

Track completion with a simple LMS (Notion, Trainual, or iSpring) and tie training completion to specific milestones — probation review, product launch access, equipment sign-off. Accountability drives completion far more than reminders.

How often do I need to update AI-generated training materials?

Review training materials whenever your product, process, or pricing changes. The advantage of AI-generated content is how fast updates happen — edit the source document, regenerate the module, and the updated version is live immediately.

What's the fastest way to build a complete onboarding programme?

Start with Notion AI: paste your existing onboarding notes, SOPs, and process documents and ask AI to structure them into a 5-day onboarding plan. This alone can create a complete first-week programme in 2–3 hours. Add Synthesia videos for the most important topics.
